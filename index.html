CofC's CIS Program

The College of Charleston's Computer Information Systems Major prepares students to have a strong understanding of both technology and business practices. Our graduates obtain professional roles such as application developer, web developer, systems analyst , project manager, database administrator, quality assurance specialist or high-tech start-up entrepreneur. students benefit from required classes in data science, management information systems, business statist ics, accounting, economics and finance.
